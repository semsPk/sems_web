Our Mission

on a mission to help every household reduce their energy use by an average 15%, reducing Pakistans carbon footptint

Reducing household electricity usage is key to decreasing Pakistan's carbon footprint, as 59% of its electricity is generated from fossil fuels. By cutting individual consumption, we directly reduce the demand for fossil fuel-based energy, contributing to a significant decrease in overall carbon emissions. This collective effort empowers households to play a crucial role in fostering sustainability and mitigating the environmental impact of conventional energy sources.
